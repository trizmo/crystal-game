Regular Clicker!

Here are the instructions

You will be given a Random Number at the start of the game

There are four Regular Show Characters below. By clicking on one of them you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. Benson fires you for going over that number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each character will have a different value for each round.


good luck!
